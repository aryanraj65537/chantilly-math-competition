Competition Information

The Chantilly Math Competition is an annual math competition for elementary and middle school students hosted by Chantilly Math Club.

Schedule

The competition will be on May 23, 2025. The schedule is shown below.

Time Event
4:30PM-5:00PM Check-In
5:00PM-5:20PM Opening Ceremony
5:20PM-7:00PM Competition
7:00PM-7:30PM Pizza Time
7:30PM-8:00PM Closing Ceremony

Location

The competition will be held in the Chantilly High School cafeteria, and our address is 4201 Stringfellow Rd, Chantilly, VA 20151.

Eligibility

The competition is open to all elementary and middle school students who can commute to Chantilly High School on the date of the competition.

Format

There are two forms—one intended for students in 5th grade and below (ES) and another for students in grades 6–8 (MS). Please note that students in 5th grade and below may take either form, but students in grades 6–8 are required to take the MS form. The competition consists of 25 questions that collectively cover algebra, combinatorics, geometry, and number theory. Competitors will be given 100 minutes to solve the problems. The point value of each problem varies based on difficulty and will be displayed on the test forms.
