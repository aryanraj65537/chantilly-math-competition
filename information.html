Competition Information

The Chantilly Math Competition is an annual math competition for elementary and middle school students hosted by Chantilly Math Club. Its purpose is to stimulate interest in mathematics by providing an enriching experience for competitors. We are pleased to announce that the 4th Chantilly Math Competition will be taking place in March 2025! The details of the event can be found below:

Date & Time

TBD

Location

Chantilly High School, 4201 Stringfellow Rd, Greenbriar, VA 20151

Format

There are two forms–one intended for students in 6th grade and below (ES) and another for students in grades 7–8 (MS). Please note that students in 6th grade and below may take either form, but students in grades 7–8 are required to take the MS form. The competition consists of 25 questions that collectively cover algebra, combinatorics, geometry, and number theory. The point value of each problem varies based on difficulty and will be displayed on the test forms.

Eligibility

The competition is open to all elementary and middle school students who can commute to Chantilly High School on the date of the competition.

Awards & Prizes

Top scorers will receive certificates and cash prizes!
